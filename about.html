About CyberShield AI

Learn about the mission, technology, and developer behind the project

Our Mission

CyberShield AI was built to make the internet a safer place by leveraging the power of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. We believe that automated content moderation tools should be accessible, open-source, and effective for communities of all sizes.

The project focuses on detecting cyberbullying patterns in social media comments, messages, and posts — providing instant classification and actionable insights for moderators and platform owners.

The Technology

CyberShield AI combines traditional machine learning with modern NLP techniques:

TF-IDF Vectorization Logistic Regression VADER Sentiment NLTK Tokenization Streamlit UI Plotly Charts pandas numpy

The model is trained on a curated educational dataset with 60+ labeled samples across three categories: Safe, Toxic, and Severe Toxic.
